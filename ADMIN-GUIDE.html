📰 Posting News & Announcements

How to post a news article
  1. Log in to admin.southwagga.com.au
  2. Click News in the left sidebar
  3. Click the + Create Item button (top right)
  4. Fill in:
    • Title — the headline
    • Hero Image — click to upload a photo (recommended)
    • Excerpt — 1–2 sentence summary (shown on news list page)
    • Body — the full article text
    • Author — your name
  5. Set Status to Published
  6. Click Save — the site updates automatically
💡 Auto-post to Facebook & Instagram: Make sure "Post to Socials" is turned ON (it is by default). The article will automatically post to the Warriors Facebook page and Instagram when you publish.
Editing or unpublishing an article
  1. Go to News → click the article title
  2. Make your changes and click Save
  3. To hide an article: change Status to Archived and Save

📷 Adding Photos to Gallery

Create a new photo album
  1. Go to Gallery Albums in the sidebar
  2. Click + Create Item
  3. Fill in: Title (e.g. "Round 14 — Warriors vs Collingullie"), Date, and optionally link to a Team
  4. Upload a Cover Photo — this is shown in the gallery grid
  5. Set Status to Published and Save
  6. Now go to Gallery Photos → + Create Item
  7. Select the Album you just created
  8. Upload your photos one at a time (or ask Andy to bulk upload)
💡 Best photo size: 1200×800px or larger. JPG or WebP format. The site automatically resizes them.

👤 Managing Players & Squads

Add a new player
  1. Go to Players → + Create Item
  2. Fill in: First Name, Last Name, Photo, Jersey Number, Position, Team
  3. Set Status to Active and Save
The player appears on their team's page automatically.
Mark a player as captain
  1. Open the player's record
  2. Toggle Is Captain to ON
  3. Save — a "C" badge will appear on their photo on the team page
Remove a player (end of season / left club)
  1. Open the player's record
  2. Change Status to Inactive
  3. Save — they'll disappear from the team page but their record is kept

🤝 Managing Sponsors

Add a new sponsor
  1. Go to Sponsors → + Create Item
  2. Fill in: Name, Logo (upload), Website URL, Tier (Platinum/Gold/Silver/Bronze)
  3. Is Global ON = appears on the main Sponsors page
  4. To link to specific teams: go to that team's record (under Teams) and add the sponsor in the Sponsors field
  5. Save

🎓 Academy Programs

Add or update an academy program
  1. Go to Academy Programs → + Create Item (or click an existing one)
  2. Fill in: Name, Age Group, Description, Schedule, Price, Price Note
  3. Square URL: paste the checkout link from your Square dashboard
  4. Status options: Active (open for enrolment), Full (shows "join waitlist"), Inactive (hidden)
  5. Save
💡 To get a Square checkout link: log in to Square → Items → click the item → Share → Copy link

🛒 Shop Items

Add a shop item
  1. Go to Shop Items → + Create Item
  2. Fill in: Name, Image, Description, Price, Category, Square URL
  3. Optional: add a Badge label (e.g. "NEW", "POPULAR")
  4. Status: Active = visible, Out of Stock = shown but greyed out, Hidden = not shown

🎟 Events & Tickets

Add an event (e.g. presentation night)
  1. Go to Events → + Create Item
  2. Fill in: Name, Image, Date, Venue, Description, Price, Square URL
  3. Toggle Is Featured ON to highlight it on the events page
  4. Set Status to Published and Save

⚽ Results & Fixtures

Results update automatically

You don't need to do anything. Results, fixtures, and league tables are pulled from fww.dribl.com automatically every 2 hours.

On Saturday and Sunday game days, they update every hour from midday onwards.

✓ As long as results are entered in Dribl, they'll appear on the Warriors website automatically.

⚙ Site Settings

Update contact details, tagline, or social links
  1. Go to Site Settings in the sidebar (there's only one record)
  2. Update any fields: Club Name, Tagline, Email, Phone, Address, Facebook URL, Instagram Handle
  3. Save — changes appear on the website within 30 seconds

🔧 Troubleshooting

ProblemWhat to do
Site not updating after I savedWait 60 seconds and hard-refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R). If still not updated, contact Andy.
Results not showingCheck fww.dribl.com is showing results. The sync runs every 2 hours — results from a game may take up to 2 hours to appear.
Photo won't uploadCheck file size is under 20MB. Use JPG or PNG format. Try a different browser.
Article not auto-posting to FacebookCheck "Post to Socials" toggle is ON. Contact Andy if Facebook connection needs re-authorising.
Can't log in to adminUse the "Forgot password" link on the login page, or contact Andy.
Square link not workingLog in to Square dashboard and check the item is still active. Copy a fresh checkout link.
